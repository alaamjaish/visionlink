The agent's brain. Tells it who it is, when to call tools, and how to speak. Reset button restores the prompt the project shipped with.
With our hot I2S mic, HIGH end-sensitivity + ~500ms silence works. Drop end-sensitivity to LOW only if Gemini cuts you off mid-sentence.
gpt-realtime-2 supports configurable reasoning effort. low is OpenAI's recommended default. Use high for hard multi-step requests.
Default 1.2 — gpt-realtime-2's voices speak deliberately at 1.0 (sounds slow word-by-word in industrial demos). 1.2 is snappier without sounding chipmunky. Crank to 1.3-1.4 for very fast delivery. Applies on the next START LIVE.
Higher threshold → less likely to false-trigger on background noise. 0.5 is a sane default; raise to 0.7 in noisy factory.
