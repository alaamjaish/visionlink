Sustainable Development Goals

VisionLink genuinely addresses five SDGs.

SDG 3

Good Health & Well-being

SOS panic mode replaces a verbal shout-for-help with a structured incident: safety officer emailed instantly, AI-guided conversation, photos auto-snapped every 10 s without the worker operating the device.

SDG 4

Quality Education

Documentation Session, Voice Note, and Photo/Video turn every shift into a knowledge-creation event, converting tacit shop-floor expertise into searchable artefacts.

SDG 8

Decent Work & Economic Growth

Hands-free wearable + AI assistance reduces physical risk and cognitive load. Supervisor dashboard gives real-time visibility into safety events.

SDG 9 & 12

Industry, Innovation & Responsible Consumption

A concrete Industry 4.0 artefact bridging the factory floor to cloud AI. AI-mediated parts requests and incident logging create a structured trail that replaces ad-hoc verbal requests.
